Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic server logs (request timestamps, error codes) for debugging purposes.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate you and display your personal expense history.
  • Generate summaries and breakdowns within the app.
  • Diagnose technical issues and maintain service reliability.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating Spendly for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secured database accessible only to you and, where technically necessary, to the service operator. We apply standard security practices including access controls and encrypted connections. No data is shared with third parties unless required by law.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly currently uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Google Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN for typography; no personal data is transmitted as part of this request beyond your IP address, which is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request deletion of your data, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
